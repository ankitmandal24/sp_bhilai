About US

Superposition is an international non-profit bridging the gender gap in STEM through events, chapters, and mentorship. Our mission is to empower women in STEM by creating learning opportunities and supportive spaces for pursuing their passions.

Since 2016, we've held the Bay Area's largest gender-focused hackathon in partnership with companies like VSCO and Uber. In March 2020, we expanded beyond a 24-hour hackathon to provide year-round opportunities for our community, such as our Uber mentorship programs and chapter program.

Now, Superposition is a global organization with 120 chapters in 25 countries. Get involved with Superposition by joining our Slack or starting a chapter!
