About US

Superposition is an international non-profit bridging the gender gap in STEM. Our mission to empower women in STEM by creating educational opportunities and supportive environments in which they can develop their passions.
Every year, we host the Bay Area's largest all-women and non-binary hackathon. This February, we hosted 220 attendees at Uber HQ in San Francisco. Recently, we've expanded beyond a 24-hour hackathon to provide year-round opportunities for our community, such as a virtual webinar series and chapter program. With our events, content, and 23 chapters, we've reached 100,000+ individuals in 51+ countries.

Women make up only 28% of the STEM workforce. Superposition bhilai is founded by Mrs Khushi Gupta in 2022 and seeks to bridge that gender gap by empowering young women in STEM through educational opportunities and supportive environments .
With Founder Miss Khushi Gupta and Co-Founder Dr. Sidharta Choubey, there are 36 members working in Superposition Bhilai Chapter.
